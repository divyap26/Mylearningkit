About Mylearningkits

Mylearningkits is an initiative to revaluate the experimental education system of schools which will encapsulate the culture of science in students with help of new aids of electronics technologies. In order to mitigate the gap between the student's concepts & creativity with modern industry visualization, we adhere the protocols of industry with realistic design of experimental kits on printed circuit board. Specialty of design is that there is no requirement of sharp mechanical tools and soldering activity by just providing push-fit mechanism to implement experiments. Kits are designed to provide experimental demonstration of laws and theorem of school courses interactively so that students can learn theorems, laws, rules and logics by just playing as a game.
